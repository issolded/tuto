tuto — “My Homework”, three ages

A new home tile where a kid photographs finished homework and sends it to Tuto. Tuto checks it in the background and forwards it to the parent; the reward stays pending until the parent approves. The full loop — Home → add photos → sent for approval — is clickable in each skin. Add a couple of photos, hit send.

Tap the My Homework tile on any phone. ★ Photos are striped placeholders standing in for real camera shots.

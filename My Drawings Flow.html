tuto — “My Drawings”, three ages

A new home tile: the kid browses drawing goals, picks one (say a cat), gets a get-your-pencil-ready screen, then follows step-by-step guided drawing (2/5, back & next) until the picture is complete. Then they photograph their real drawing and it lands — with an instant reward — in their My Paintings library, dated. The full loop is clickable in each skin.

Each phone opens on the child's home page — see the new My Drawings tile in the grid (next to My Homework). Tap it → pick a target (or “Draw my own idea”) → follow the steps → add a photo. ★ The drawing character on the tile & card changes by age: cat (6–8), cute otter (9–11), cool anime (12–15). Step sketches & photo are placeholders.

My Tree · growth model

How the tree grows across a month

Growth follows approved leaves, not the calendar — action is rewarded, missed days never shrink the tree. A soft daily cap keeps it growing all month instead of maxing out in one big day. Four silhouettes mark the milestones; the size eases up continuously in between, so every single leaf is felt.

🌳 Full tree — Ages 6–8: 12 leaves Ages 9–11: 18 leaves Ages 12–15: 24 leaves
🍃Leaves, not days. One approved contribution = one leaf. The plant grows from what the child does.
⏳Soft daily cap (~2/day). Extra entries still log & celebrate, but growth paces out over the 30 days.
🌸Past full, it blooms. Contributions beyond the target turn to blossom — then the tree joins the forest at month-end.

My Tree → My Part — a diary of things I did to help

The child logs kind things they did — at home or out in the world. A parent approves each one. Every entry grows a leaf; every month starts a new tree; the trees become a forest to look back on. One screen, three age shells — same layout & components, the visual weight, tone and title shift with age. No points or rewards live here — those happen monthly, elsewhere. Mock data · category & moderation are backend, hinted only by colour.

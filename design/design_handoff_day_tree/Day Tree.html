One tree a day, a forest by month's end

Each day grows its own tree: a morning sprout that fills out with every contribution. Once a day's tree is full, extra help doesn't vanish — it adds fruit, then blossom, then butterflies, so the 8th task of the day still makes a visible change. At day's end the tree is planted into the month forest — nothing is ever lost, and the long arc is the forest filling up. Tap the cards to watch today's tree grow past full.
